Our Story

Born in London.
Brought Home
by Venkat.

When Venkat moved to the UK, he fell in love with a dessert unlike anything he had tasted before — intensely rich chocolate dessert that Londoners had been obsessing over for years. He spent over two years in the UK for his studies, besides which he understood the recipe and perfected every detail.

When he came back home, he brought one thing with him: the original Dolcericco recipe. His mission was simple — Tirupati deserves to taste what the world has been celebrating. No shortcuts, no compromises. The same London quality, right here in your city.

Dolcericco Tirupati is the first and only place in the entire region where you can taste this legendary dessert — made fresh, on order, every single time.
